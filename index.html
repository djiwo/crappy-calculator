Why this app?

For anyone who wants to use a very basic calculator.

I originally made this app in my spare time, with my statistics students in mind. They have to use a crappy calculator, exactly like this one, on their exam. This app can help students to get used to working with these old scool calculators.
The biggest hurdle is working without brackets (Hi there, old-school order of operations!)

Of course, you could just not use the brackets on your fancy calculator. But face it. You're human. And we're not made to resist temptation. If there's ice cream, you'll eat it. Ergo, if there are brackets on your calculator, you'll use them. Save yourself the agony: Use this crappy calculator instead.
